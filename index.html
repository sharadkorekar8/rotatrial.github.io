    • COMMUNITY SERVICE

    This committee plans and implements activities or service projects to create sustainable change that directly benefits the local or university community, involving all or most of the club membership.

    • PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT

    This committee develops a comprehensive professional development program designed to expand professional networks, allow members to exchange ideas with other leaders, and build skills through leadership training.

    • INTERNATIONAL SERVICE

    This committee identifies opportunities in order to plan and implement activities or service projects to create sustainable change that directly benefits the international community, involving all or most of the club membership.

    As we embark on this new rotary year 2019-2020 we are reminded by our President of Rotary International, Mark Daniel Maloney, that 'Rotary Connects the World'. In fact, “Rotary Connects the World” is this year’s theme of Rotary International. In the words of President Maloney, “Rotary allows us to connect with one another, in deep and meaningful ways, across our differences. It connects us with people we would never otherwise have met, who are more like us than we ever could have known. It connects us with our communities, to professional opportunities, and to the people who need our help.”

    Trust me it’s an absolute honor and privilege to stand before you as the President of this club for the year 2019-2020. I thank the past President for putting their best efforts to bring the club to this height. Being fully aware of the fact that I may not reach upto his level. I promise the members of this club and this eminent gathering that I will put all my knowledge and my efforts to ensure that the good work is continued. We shall be aligned with the priorities set forth by the Rotary International and the instrumental in contributing to the world community.

    we will be organizing the Industrial Visits for each Engineering Department of our college in this year. We will also organize the cleanliness drive and the Tree Plantation Program which is the top priority need of this society. We will conduct the Seminar on the Leadership Skills which will help us throughout the life. We will be arranging the Guidance Lecture on the Civil Service Exams, and other exams which will help the students. It gives me an immense pleasure to announce that we are working on Android Application of our club which will help the students to register to our club and get the updates of the club as well as it will also help us to reach to the students. I am proud to inform you that this Application will be made by the Members of our Club itself by the Students from Computer Engineering Department.

    I look forward for all your guidance and support as we drive our club from here and let you see the great things that we do with it. I would like to conclude my words with quoting

    ‘We Can and We Will’.
    Thank You.
